Last Tuesday morning I was a bit busy.. having my third *(and final)* beautiful little girl!

![Little Mira looks away, in wonder](/images/Mira/index.jpg)

Born in the labor tub at the local hospital, she swam out happily at around 2am, less than two hours after getting there. We kept the gender a surprise, and as such, the name was up in the air. After much deliberation, on the following day I was finally given the go ahead to name her. I racked my brain and finally came up with Mirabelle, or Mira for short.

Also to be announced, she'll be joining us on our trip to Maker Fair Bay Area this year, and oh it will be a good one!

Now to get some rest...
