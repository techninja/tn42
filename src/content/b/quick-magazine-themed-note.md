Alright, not to make an overly big deal out of this, but after a casual comment on [an excellent Mythbusters interview over on Popular Mechanics](http://www.popularmechanics.com/home_journal/workshop/4332948.html), and a tweet over to it's author, the fabulous [Erin McCarthy](http://twitter.com/erincmccarthy), she _actually_ replied to me. Not only that, she then proceeded to offer me the guest edited Mythbusters issue I'd missed. _No freakin' way!_

![The issue as sent amid the cruft on my desk. Erin also included this nice thank you card as well. Sylvia jumped up and down!](/images/PM_Awesome/index.jpg)

Do _you_ know any other Hearst owned 1.2 Mil.+ circulation full glossy publication where the assistant editor will sweetly tweet back and simply send you a previous issue, no cost? **_Do you?!_**

Hats off to Popular Mechanics Assistant Editor, Erin McCarthy, and the whole team over at PM for pure professionalism in, what it seems, all they do. Not to mention, they seem to have good taste in both assistant, _and_ guest editors.
