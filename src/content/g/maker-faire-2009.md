If you can rate a singular location in terms of how many times you say "Wow", "Oh my god", "Holy Crap", "That's insane" and "Freakin' hell that's *robotic* **and** on fire!!"... Then Maker Faire 2009 rates at least in the 200-300 range.

In 2006, I could only stay for the first day. This year I stayed and walked for the entirety of both days.. even staying at least an extra hour after it was meant to close. My daughter and I met [ladyada](http://www.adafruit.com/), [kipkay](http://www.kipkay.com/), [adam savage](http://twitter.com/donttrythis) and so many other amazing makers there. I also saw many burning man favorites from [cyclecide](http://www.cyclecide.com/) to [The Golden Mean (The Snail Car)](http://www.flickr.com/photos/laughingsquid/2932748872/).

I couldn't even begin to describe the pure vastness (it took up the entire San Mateo fairgrounds) and amazingness of it all. I was on my feet for a calculated total of 18 hours, with schedule in hand I hoofed it to every corner of the place, and I think I still missed over 20% of it... ARgH!!

Here's the first of a small selection of photos I took from the event.. I plan on posting one more batch here, and the full set on flickr.
